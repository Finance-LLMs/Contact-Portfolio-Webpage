Our Winning Solution

Problem Statement

Enhancing Retail Investor Education and Engagement

Many retail investors lack the knowledge to navigate the securities market, leading to poor investment decisions or reliance on unverified advice. Further, most online sources of information for retail investors are in English, creating a lack of educative material in various vernacular languages.

Challenge: Develop an interactive app that educates users on stock market basics, risk assessment techniques, algo trading/HFT, and portfolio diversification using delayed market data and simulated trading scenarios.

Our Solution

Indian Finance Investors Dashboard

A multilingual, voice-enabled AI platform that uses video avatars and natural conversation through the ElevenLabs SDK. It provides accessible, educational financial consultations‚Äîideal for retail investors. With added Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), the AI can serve up-to-date, context-rich insights.

Tech Stack: AI/ML (Conversational AI, RAG, multilingual LLM agents), ElevenLabs SDK, Node.js/Express, FastAPI, Vanilla JavaScript, CSS3, HTML5
Languages: English, Hindi, Tamil

Key Features & Innovation

  • Multilingual Conversational AI - Breaking language barriers in financial education
  • Voice & Video Interaction - Real-time conversations with AI avatars
  • Multiple Financial Advisors - Different investment philosophies and expertise
  • RAG Integration - Up-to-date, context-rich financial insights
  • SEBI-Compliant - Educational focus with proper disclaimers
  • Responsive Design - Accessible on all devices
Differentiator: The first multilingual, voice-and-video AI dashboard that makes financial literacy engaging, interactive, and SEBI-compliant.

Impact & Scalability

Democratizes financial education by breaking language barriers and making expert-level financial knowledge accessible to underserved retail investors across India's expanding investment ecosystem.
