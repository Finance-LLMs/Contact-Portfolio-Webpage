Winning Solution

Indian Finance Investors Dashboard Revolutionizing Financial Education Through AI

A multilingual, voice-enabled AI platform that democratizes financial knowledge and makes expert-level education accessible to every retail investor across India.

Challenge

Problem Statement

Understanding the critical gaps in retail investor education

Enhancing Retail Investor Education and Engagement

Key Challenges

  • • Many retail investors lack knowledge to navigate securities markets
  • • Poor investment decisions due to inadequate education
  • • Reliance on unverified advice and misinformation
  • • Most educational content available only in English
  • • Lack of engaging, interactive learning platforms

Requirements

  • • Interactive app for stock market basics education
  • • Risk assessment techniques training
  • • Algorithmic trading and HFT concepts
  • • Portfolio diversification strategies
  • • Multilingual support for vernacular languages
  • • Simulated trading scenarios and delayed market data
Innovation

Our Solution

A comprehensive AI-powered platform addressing all identified challenges

AI-Powered Multilingual Financial Education Platform

Finance-Investors-Dashboard is a groundbreaking multilingual, voice-enabled AI platform that uses video avatars and natural conversation through the ElevenLabs SDK. It provides accessible, educational financial consultations—ideal for retail investors seeking to improve their financial literacy.

Core Innovation

  • • Real-time voice synthesis via ElevenLabs SDK
  • • Interactive video avatars for engagement
  • • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for up-to-date insights
  • • Multiple AI advisor personas with different investment philosophies
  • • SEBI-compliant educational framework

Target Impact

  • • Break language barriers in financial education
  • • Democratize access to expert financial knowledge
  • • Enhance retail investor confidence and decision-making
  • • Support India's expanding investment ecosystem
  • • Promote financial inclusion across diverse communities
